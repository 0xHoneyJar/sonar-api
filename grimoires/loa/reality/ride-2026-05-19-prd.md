## 1. Product Overview

freeside-sonar is the THJ ecosystem's on-chain data backbone — a multi-chain
blockchain event indexer that turns raw contract events into a queryable GraphQL
API. [GROUNDED: `README.md`, `config.yaml`, `schema.graphql`]

It is the **single source of truth** for downstream THJ apps: CubQuests, Score API,
Set & Forgetti, ApiologyDAO governance, and the Mibera substrate. [GROUNDED:
`README.md`; `src/lib/actions.ts` quest-log; `SFPosition`/`Apdao*` entities]

## 3. Features (As Built)

### F1 — Multi-Chain NFT Tracking
Tracks NFT transfers, mints, and burns for HoneyJar Gen1-6, Honeycomb, Mibera,
Milady, Crayons, and generic tracked collections across 6 chains, aggregating
holders cross-chain. [GROUNDED: `src/handlers/honey-jar-nfts.ts`,
`mibera-collection.ts`, `milady-collection.ts`, `tracked-erc721.ts`;
`ADDRESS_TO_COLLECTION` in `handlers/constants.ts`]

### F2 — Universal Quest / Action Log
A uniform `Action` entity records actor + actionType + collection + context for
every quest-relevant event, consumed by CubQuests. [GROUNDED: `src/lib/actions.ts:103`
`recordAction`; `schema.graphql:1` `Action`]

### F3 — Vault Indexing (Moneycomb, Set & Forgetti, Henlo, Aquabera)
Indexes ERC4626 and custom vault contracts: deposits, withdrawals, shares,
rewards, rounds, strategy registration. [GROUNDED: `src/handlers/moneycomb-vault.ts`,
`sf-vaults.ts`, `henlo-vault.ts`, `aquabera-vault-direct.ts`]

### F4 — fatBERA & Validator Staking
Tracks fatBERA deposits, validator deposits/withdrawals, block rewards, and
withdrawal batching. [GROUNDED: `src/handlers/fatbera.ts`]

### F5 — Mibera Substrate
Indexes Mibera premints, staking, ERC1155 sets (OP/Zora), and liquid-backing /
RFV snapshots. [GROUNDED: `src/handlers/mibera-premint.ts`, `mibera-staking.ts`,
`mibera-sets.ts`, `mibera-zora.ts`, `mibera-liquid-backing.ts`]

### F6 — ApiologyDAO Governance
Indexes the ApiologyDAO auction house: auctions, bids, settlements, queued tokens.
[GROUNDED: `src/handlers/apdao-auction.ts`; `Apdao*` entities in `schema.graphql`]

### F7 — DeFi & Marketplace Tracking
Indexes PaddleFi lending, friend.tech shares trades, Mirror writing-edition
purchases, BGT boosts, and (config-disabled) Seaport orders. [GROUNDED:
`src/handlers/paddlefi.ts`, `friendtech.ts`, `mirror-observability.ts`, `bgt.ts`;
`seaport.ts` — contract commented out in `config.yaml`]

### F8 — Aggregate Stats
Maintains mutable aggregate-stat entities (collection supply, holder counts, burn
totals, trade stats) alongside immutable event entities. [GROUNDED:
`CollectionStat`, `GlobalCollectionStat`, `*Stats` entities in `schema.graphql`]
